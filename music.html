Bio

Tom Ingvorsen is a Jazz drummer and composer born in West Berkshire, UK. Tom started playing drums at the age
of 9, which kickstarted a lifelong love of music, leading him to a career as a Jazz drummer/band leader after
graduating from the Royal Birmingham Conservatoire in 2022. Tom also cites influences from a variety of other
musical genres in his compositions, including Progressive Rock and Scandinavian Folk, which has lead him to the
contemporary Jazz style he has come to be known for.
